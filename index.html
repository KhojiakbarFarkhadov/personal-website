01.About me

I'm a Middle Technical Writer with a strong software engineering background (.NET, PHP, JavaScript) and more than five years of experience building — and explaining — complex software systems.

My core focus is developer- and user-facing documentation: REST API references, integration guides, BRDs, user manuals, release notes, and internal knowledge bases. I work in docs-as-code workflows using Git and Markdown, and collaborate closely with engineers, analysts, and product teams to keep documentation accurate, up to date, and easy to use.

What makes my writing different is simple: I've spent years on the other side of the docs. I've designed the APIs, written the stored procedures, and debugged the integrations — so when I document a system, I document it the way an engineer actually needs it.

Currently I work at FIDO-BIZNES, where I design and maintain documentation for financial and business applications, including detailed API docs and Business Requirement Documents.
